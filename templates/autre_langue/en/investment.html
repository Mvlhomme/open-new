OPEN BANK INTERNATIONAL is a credit institution and investment services provider founded in October 1992 with a European banking license. Our mission is to provide European households with transparent and competitive consumer loans, financed directly by professional investors *. Our 100% digital platform features cutting-edge technologies allowing us to offer competitive interest rates to borrowers and attractive returns to investors.

We invest in depreciable household loans at fixed rate and constant monthly payments. The initial loan maturities are between 6 and 84 months, granted via the OPEN BANK INTERNATIONAL platform. Our portfolio of investments aims to provide stable and competitive returns with low volatility.
